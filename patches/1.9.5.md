# New Panel: Chunk Map

Interactive OSRS world map for chunk-locked accounts and series planning.

## Chunk Map

- **Shift+click to unlock chunks** on the full OSRS world map with pan and zoom
- **Glow and border effects** with independent colour, thickness, and style controls
- **30+ marker icons** including skills, quests, minigames, achievement diaries, and more
- **Stack multiple icons** on a single chunk with a card-fan layout
- **Alt+click to add text labels** to any chunk with auto-shrinking OSRS font
- **Animated chunk reveal** with queue system for recording or presentations
- **One-click PNG export** of your current view
- **All settings saved automatically** and persist between sessions
